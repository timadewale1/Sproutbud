Our Impact

SproutBud creates measurable change by training young people, supporting women entrepreneurs, mobilizing community action on waste, and delivering technology and clean energy solutions that expand opportunity across Nigeria and Africa.

Training & Skills

Trained 180+ students in digital literacy, reached 92 teachers, and supported tech incubation cohorts for young founders.

Community & Environment

Mobilized volunteers to remove 2 tonnes of waste; formed Community Waste Watch Groups and ran national cleanup events.

Women’s Empowerment

Delivered the Young Women’s Entrepreneurship Launchpad and W.O.M.E.N legal and health services for vulnerable women.

Selected Highlights

  • Trained 180+ children and youth in digital literacy and tech skills.
  • Launched the Young Women’s Entrepreneurship Launchpad and selected businesses for mentorship.
  • Mobilized 57 volunteers to remove 2 tonnes of waste through World Cleanup Day activities.
  • Provided pro bono legal aid and health services through the W.O.M.E.N Initiative.
