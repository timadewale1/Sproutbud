WHO WE ARE
SproutBud Ltd/Gte is a dynamic, youth-led social impact organization operating across innovation, climate action, clean energy access, gender equity, and sustainable community development. We create inclusive, scalable solutions that empower youth, women, and communities to thrive.


OUR MISSION
To nurture grassroots innovation, expand clean energy access, empower underserved communities, and drive inclusive development across Africa through transformative programs in technology, climate action, enterprise support, and women’s rights.


OUR VISION
A Nigeria where youth, women, and communities thrive through technology, clean energy, green innovation, and social justice.


OUR CORE VALUES
* Innovation: Embracing creative, tech-driven solutions

* Equity: Centering women, youth, and marginalized voices

* Sustainability: Building economically and ecologically resilient communities

* Collaboration: Partnering with communities, organizations, and governments

* Impact: Committing to measurable, positive change
