Who We Are

SproutBud is a youth-led African social impact organization harnessing innovation to solve community challenges across technology, climate action, clean energy, women empowerment, and agriculture.

Founded in 2018, we've grown from a small volunteer group to a pan-African movement with operations in 12 countries and over 50,000 youth members.
