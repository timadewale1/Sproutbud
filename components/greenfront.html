About GreenFront

GreenFront is our environmental sustainability program that addresses climate change through community-led conservation, renewable energy adoption, and environmental education.

Since 2019, we've planted over 500,000 trees, trained 8,000 climate ambassadors, and implemented 45 community conservation projects across 10 African countries.

Climate literacy programs for schools and communities.

Youth-led climate policy engagement and activism.
