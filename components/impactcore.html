About ImpactCore

ImpactCore is our flagship technology program that establishes digital innovation hubs aross Africa to equip youth with digital skills and support tech-driven solutions to community challenges.

Since 2018, we've launched 15 innovation hubs in 8 countries, trained over 12,000 youth in digital skills, and incubated 120 tech solutions addressing local challenges.
