About Innovate

Innovate is our clean energy and sustainable technology program that develops and deploys solutions to address energy poverty and environmental challenges across Africa.

Since 2020, we've trained 5,000 youth in clean energy technologies, supported 75 energy startups, and brought renewable energy solutions to 300 rural communities.
